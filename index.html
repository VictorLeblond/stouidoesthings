Explo bot

explo bot is a game I made during the weekend of the cs games 2023, I then polished it (background, art++, menu) during the next summer and published it on itch.io 

this game is a platformer to the liking of jump king, the goal is to go up and falling doesn't kill your character, but maybe you from the inside

I plan on making a dedicated page for this game on this website, just like the 2024 version

made with unity and aseprite, help with sound from friends



Dream Catchers

this was a project made with 8 other university students at udem, we had 8 weeks to make a 10 min long game about dreams

I was assigned in art, but I took part in every sphere of the making of this game

the result is a neat coop puzzle platformer where you play two penguins trying to recollect fragment of dreams

your mission is to go up, pushing blocks to make bridges and collecting dreams

the contest had request such as ; the implementation of an AI actor, online local coop, has to have an element draining over time (collecting dreams kept the game going)

having openned this website, I'd like to go back to this project and make a playable version of everybody...

3D RPG Prototype

this project was a prototype of my vision of how I would make a game like WoW and dark souls combined

it featured the attack combos of dark souls with the ability / inventory system of your typical MMORPG

it was a great learning experience; I understood how much time making animated 3D assets took or how to build robusts systems to allow the creation of new abilities-weapons-items easily

made in unity, blender, aseprite, VCV rack

this kind of project makes me want to create a whole digital world with unique atmospheres and details through video games

A* pathfinding on enemies
character is able to use combo animations
inventory system
music created in VCV rack
