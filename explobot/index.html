Explobot

Explobot is a game I started during a weekend of Febuary 2023. I polished it during the summer of 2023.

My goal for the project was to have a complete but simple game within a short period of time.
The game is about a little robot going up to plug himself as he is running out of battery. It's played using only the mouse.
