These assets were for a game I worked on in 2018-2019. The game was an roguelike bullethell action rpg similar to Enter The Gungeon.
This project, even unfinished, means a lot to me as it was a turning point into my pixel art style. I was able to learn a lot and touch
many aspects of making assets for a 2D game (Characters, Tilemaps, etc...)
Being 16 at the time, I had to stop working on the project as the scope of it was very big and I was working on it solo.
There is a workable version of the game, all the mechanics are implemented, so I may come back to it and polish it.
